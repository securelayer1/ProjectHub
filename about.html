About Me

Hi, I’m Adri. After a series of life experiences that pushed me out of my comfort zone, I decided it was time for a real change — not just small adjustments, but a complete reset. I moved to a new country, stepped into the unknown, and started the process of reinventing myself. It hasn’t been easy. There were moments of loneliness, doubt, and questioning everything I thought I knew about myself. But in that space, I began to grow. I started trying things that once intimidated me, doing things I used to believe I wasn’t capable of. Little by little, I’m learning, exploring, and discovering new sides of who I am. This is a personal project — a space where I share my journey, my ideas, and my growth. Not a finished story, but an honest process of becoming.
